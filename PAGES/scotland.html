Scotland

One more country cleared for the count. I really enjoyed walking around the city of Edinburgh and visiting Edinburgh Castle

Loch Ness

I visited the Famous Loch Ness is world renowned for the alleged apparitions of the Loch Ness Monster, known to locals as Nessie. The scientific evidence for the existence of this monster, described as a kind of sea dragon, is almost null. I at least did not encounter the Loch Ness Monster this time. Who knows next time?

Edinburgh Castle

One of the country's most important castles, and the second most visited tourist attraction in Scotland, welcoming around one million people annually. It was amazing to see how big this castle is.

Edinburgh Streets

I walked the streets of Edinburgh where they call the medieval old town and an elegant Georgian new town that includes gardens and neoclassical buildings..

Princes Street Gardens - Edinburgh

A square near Edinburgh Castle where people walk and run for exercise. It's a very pleasant place full of greenery. And you can see Edinburgh Castle from down here.
