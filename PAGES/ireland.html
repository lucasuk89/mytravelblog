CS Lewis Square - Belfast

For those who have watched or read the fictional works of "The Chronicles of Narnia" this is a nice gift. This is a square where there are several sculptures about the movie. The reason for this is that the writer lived there at the time and wrote her work, and they decided to pay homage to her work.

Titanic Museum in Belfast

This is the titanic museum where you can find many objects that were inside the titanic. You will also find out how it was built and its history. And next to the museum is the HUGE space where it was actually built.

Game of Thrones Tour

This place is where "Aria" the iconic character from Game of Thrones crossed a lake to reach the town of "Bravos". She was on this staircase.

Game of Thrones Tour

Paintings scattered throughout the city of Belfast as a tribute to the GOT series, which was recorded in various locations around the city.
